Ruckus Wireless: The Smart Wi-Fi Antenna Technology That Steers Signals to Users.

  • [View]

Abstract

For greater speed, fewer errors, and instant bandwidth delivery, BeamFlex offers first-of-its-kind smart antenna technology that maximizes signal coverage, throughput, and network capacity.  BeamFlex consists of a smart, compact antenna array with multiple elements, which can be combined in real time to form unique antenna patterns.  The advanced BeamFlex system software continually learns the environment with all its hostilities and interference sources, including disruptive RF conditions, numerous communicating devices, network performance issues, and application flows. Then, it selects the optimum antenna pattern for each communicating device in real time, while actively avoiding interference and minimizing noise nearby networks and devices.

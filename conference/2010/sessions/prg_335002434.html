It's So Hard When Contemplated In Advance And So Easy When You Do It

Brad Feld, Foundry Group, USA

As museums move into a new era of engaging with their audiences, some of the best sources of inspiration and examples may come from outside the field. Focusing on how visitors engage and re-imagining the innovative process – essentially acting as a technology startup in the museum – museums can find new partners and new techniques to build long-term relationships and relevance with their visitors.

Distributing a Licensed Resource to Schools and Libraries

Sonja Staum-Kuniej , Herron School of Art Library, USA

Session: IMLS Sponsored R&D Projects

This paper presents the experience of the IUPUI/AMICO project which is establishing methods to enable university subscribers to the Art Museum Image Consortium to serve as distributors to the schools and public libraries in their communities. It will report on the plans for development of curriculum materials, experiences in crafting user licenses for the school and library community, and technical challenges of delivering large multimedia databases.

Title:Mobile Technologies and Cultural Institutions: A Design Perspective
Authors:Davide Spallazzo
Type:Paper
Publication:MWF2014: Museums and the Web Florence 2014
Year:2014
Abstract:

Digital technologies and mobile technologies in particular are often pointed as a driver to achieve new visiting models but their employment in cultural heritage field is anything but straightforward. The paper looks at this complex ecology from the point of view of a designer, trying to point out the main issues to be taken into account while designing a mobile experience for cultural institutions and a process that can be followed to achieve a final product from scratch.

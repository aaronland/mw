Title:Capacity Planning for Meaning
Authors:Aaron Cope
Type:Paper
Publication:MW2020: MuseWeb 2020
Year:2020
Abstract:

The purpose of a digital team inside an institution is to build and nurture the infrastructure, both technical and social, so that each subsequent project is easier than the last, or at the very least creates new challenges rather than retreading the same ground over and over again. These are precisely the sorts of things that outside agencies are not equipped to do, but the cultural heritage continues to believe they are often at the expense of resources needed to foster in-house capacity.

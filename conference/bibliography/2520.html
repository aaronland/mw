Title:Cultural Programming Using Broadband Technology
Authors:Leonard Steinbach
Type:Paper
Publication:MW2002: Museums and the Web 2002
Year:2002
Abstract:

This mini workshop will both demystify the application and potential of broadband technology. It will achieve this by explaining what it is and why it is important; helping participants identify uses that are approprate to the mission of their institutions; showing how partnerships which focus on exploiting expertise and self interest can make the difference between feasibility and futility; and, by looking at case examples on which participants can build.

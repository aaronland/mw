Title:Hints and Strategies for the Creation of the One-Person On-Line Web Museum
Authors:Richard Paselk
Type:Paper
Publication:MW2002: Museums and the Web 2002
Year:2002
Abstract:

Small museums and collections often have small or non-existent staffing and support. Collecting, preservation, restoration and repair, cataloging, photography, preparing digital images, researching, writing descriptions and essays, creating HTML, designing a web site and navigation: can (or indeed should) an individual attempt it all? This mini-workshop presentation will describe some successes and failures in just such an attempt.

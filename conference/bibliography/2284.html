Title:Conceptual and Intermedia Arts Online: The Challenge of Documenting and Presenting Non-Traditional Art Collections
Authors:Richard Rinehart
Type:Paper
Publication:MW99: Museums and the Web 1999
Year:1999
Abstract:

Conceptual & Intermedia Arts Online (Project CIAO) is an collaborative project between 7 institutions to create networked access to educational and scholarly material on the broad theme of conceptual and intermedia art. This paper outlines basic background and need for the project, some key standards being applied, and some of the unforeseen outcomes so far based on the subject-specific scope of this project.

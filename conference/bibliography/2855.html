Authors:Jesse Allison, John Fillwalk
Type:Paper
Publication:MW2009: Museums and the Web 2009
Year:2009
Abstract:

Virtual worlds provide a platform in which to construct compelling experiences not possible within the material and temporal constraints of the physical world. The virtual realm has the potential to be united and engaged by physicality

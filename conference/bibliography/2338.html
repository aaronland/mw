Title:Playing Exhibitions: Gaming On-Line and In-Gallery
Authors:Kathryn Saunders
Type:Paper
Publication:MW99: Museums and the Web 1999
Year:1999
Abstract:

Emerging Technologies and new expressive possibilities afforded by the Internet have enabled a new Museum experience: The Playing Exhibition. Kathryn Saunders and her team of researchers, exhibit designers, artists and animators are configuring exhibitions towards a new experiential authenticity. By configuring the exhibits as a game that can be played both in the gallery and on the web, visitors become the curators of their own experience.

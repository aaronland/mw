Title:Success Strategies for Engaging Audiences with Museum Website Blogs
Authors:Scott Bomboy, Rebecca Sherman
Type:Paper
Publication:Museums and the Web 2014: Selected Papers from Two International Conferences
Editors:Nancy Proctor and Rich Cherry
Pages:311–21
Publisher:Museums and the Web LLC
Published:Silver Spring
Year:2014
Abstract:

Using editorial and marketing techniques from other disciplines, bloggers on museum websites can create engaging content that attracts readers for all of a museum’s digital properties, including the Web, mobile, and tablet-based audiences.

Keywords: blog, Web, website, digital, content

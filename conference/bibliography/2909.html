Title:Caboodle by Culture24: How to Engage Children with Your Collection Online in a New Way (and How They Can Engage You in Theirs!)
Authors:Jane Finnis, Anra Kennedy
Type:Paper
Publication:MW2010: Museums and the Web 2010
Year:2010
Abstract:

The Caboodle project aims to engage children with your collection in a new way, and introduce you to their collections. In this case study we’ll look in detail at the project so far, the on-line, the off-line workshops, children’s engagement, and the lessons we’ve learned.

Title:Storytelling, Learning, and Adaptivity: A Difficult Mixing
Authors:Isabelle Astic
Type:Paper
Publication:MWF2014: Museums and the Web Florence 2014
Year:2014
Abstract:

This article analyses 3 experiments of digital adaptive interpretation tool in a museum. It shows how adaptivity influences storytelling and how it could trouble narration consistency and learning. It proposes narrative and pervasive serious game of emergence as one line of investigation to try to solve the problem. It also wonders if such games could not be a way of training to the new citizenship needed by smart-cities.

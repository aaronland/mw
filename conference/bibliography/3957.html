Title:Mapping civic art assets in Los Angeles County
Authors:Yvonne Lee, Clare Haggarty
Type:Paper
Publication:MW2016: Museums and the Web 2016
Year:2016
Abstract:

The Los Angeles County Arts Commission has embarked on its first comprehensive baseline inventory project to survey for civic artworks. This demonstration covers strategy, tools, workflow, and data management practices with an emphasis on methodology and better practices.

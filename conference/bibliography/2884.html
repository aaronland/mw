Title:Emerging Web-Browsing Alternatives
Authors:Steffen B
Type:Paper
Publication:MW2009: Museums and the Web 2009
Year:2009
Abstract:

With the constant evolution of the Web, different browsing metaphors are expanding from navigation bars, drop-down menus, and sitemaps. More recent mechanisms such as tag-browsing, timelines, calendars, and the presentation of contextualised content are being offered to support general-purpose browsing. This paper addresses how these browsing alternatives can be used to fulfill the needs of the Canadian Centre for Architecture

Title:The Infinite Museum: An Innovative Digital Platform to Transform the Museum Visitor Experience
Authors:Timothy Berg, Erin Bretz, Alyson Walbridge, Kayla Gurganus
Type:Paper
Publication:MW2015: Museums and the Web 2015
Year:2015
Abstract:

The Infinite Museum is an innovative Web application that offers museum visitors short, creative, and unusual prompts to get them thinking about the art in museums in new ways. The tool helps visitors connect their own experiences to their encounter with art and offers a powerful model for how to rethink the museum visitor experience. This demonstration and paper show how the product is used and give people a sense of the power it can unleash in a museum visitor.

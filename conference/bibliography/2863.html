Title:iTunes U
Authors:Jason Ediger, Katie Walton, Steve Wilson
Type:Paper
Publication:MW2009: Museums and the Web 2009
Year:2009
Abstract:

Museums, historical societies, public libraries and other cultural institutions have joined the hundreds of colleges and universities using iTunes U to make their educational content available to a world-wide audience. In this workshop, you will learn about iTunes U, how to prepare and present your content, and how your iTunes U site can extend your audience and further connect with your institution's patrons.

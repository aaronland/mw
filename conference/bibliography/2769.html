Title:Operation Sigismund
Authors:Dick Dijk
Type:Paper
Publication:MW2007: Museums and the Web 2007
Year:2007
Abstract:

Waag Society and the Archive of the province of Drenthe have developed Operation Sigismund. Operation Sigismund is an experiential learning environment: a magic realist representation of an archive, combining game elements, narrative elements and learning goals in a clearly defined physical space within the Archive

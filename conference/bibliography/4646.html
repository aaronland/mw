Authors:Dr. Mark Osterman
Type:Paper
Publication:MW17: Museums and the Web 2017
Year:2017
Abstract:

The presenter will discuss ways that Vizcaya increases access by designing and integrating accessible technology into the museum's interpretation and discuss the challenges faced in developing and implementing such experiences.

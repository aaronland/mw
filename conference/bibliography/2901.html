Title:Tear Down the Walls: The Re-Design of ArtsConnectEd.org
Authors:Scott Sayre, Robin Dowden
Type:Paper
Publication:MW2009: Museums and the Web 2009
Year:2009
Abstract:

In the fall of 2006, the Minneapolis Institute of Arts and Walker Art Center embarked on a large-scale redesign of their highly acclaimed Web site, ArtsConnectEd. Since its launch in 1998, ArtsConnectEd had become a core resource for K-12 educators and their students, serving well over a million users per year.Despite its accomplishments, museum educators eventually wanted better tools for adding new content, creating links between assets, and managing their relationships with ArtsConnectEd

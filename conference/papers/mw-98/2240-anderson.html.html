Opening Plenary

Maxwell Anderson, Art Gallery of Ontario

Main Session: Opening Plenary
Thursday, April 23, 1998
9:00 am - 10:30 am

Museums, separately and collectively, face exciting challenges in mastering and exploiting the Web. The Art Gallery of Ontario is a leader in many of the pioneering web implementations being developed by museums today. Its Director explores why, and how.

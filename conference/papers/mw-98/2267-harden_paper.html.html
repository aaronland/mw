Web Graphics: Art on the Net

Mark Harden, texas.net Museum of Art

Contents

  • Design considerations for an art web site
  • Evolution of a "virtual" gallery
  • Integrating Image and Text
  • Effective online display of art images
  • Limited time
  • Limited technical capability/resources
  • Unlimited content and concept

In this paper, I will offer some insight into the development of the texas.net Museum of Art. I will discuss both the limitations and the liberties associated with managing an art web site as an individual, a "netizen". This site, I feel, exemplifies the democratic ideal of the World Wide Web as a new means of expression for anyone with minimal technical skills but abundant passion and dedication.
